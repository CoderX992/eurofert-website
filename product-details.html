Crops and Usage

Suitable for vegetables, fruits, field crops, ornamentals, and greenhouse production.

Application Recommendations

Foliar Application: 2-3 kg per 1000 liters of water

Fertigation: 5-10 kg per hectare per application

Frequency: Apply every 7-14 days during active growth periods

Application Notes

Always conduct a jar test before mixing with other products. Apply during cooler parts of the day to minimize leaf burn risk. Ensure adequate water volume for uniform coverage.

Compatibility and Mixing

Compatible with most pesticides and fertilizers. Avoid mixing with highly alkaline products or those containing calcium. Always perform a jar test before tank mixing.

Storage and Safety

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep container tightly closed when not in use. Keep out of reach of children and animals. Refer to Safety Data Sheet for complete handling information.
