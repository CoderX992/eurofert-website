Description

This advanced formulation combines essential macro and micronutrients in a highly soluble and readily available form, ensuring rapid uptake and utilization by plants. The carefully balanced composition supports vigorous growth throughout all developmental stages.

Developed through extensive research and field testing, this product delivers consistent results across a wide range of soil types and climatic conditions. The unique formulation enhances nutrient efficiency while promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

Suitable for integration into modern precision agriculture systems, this product offers flexibility in application methods and timing. Whether applied through fertigation, foliar spray, or soil incorporation, it provides reliable nutrition to meet the demanding needs of high-value crops.

Application Notes

For optimal results, apply during the early morning or late evening hours to minimize evaporation and maximize nutrient uptake. Ensure adequate soil moisture before and after application to facilitate nutrient movement into the root zone.

Important Guidelines: Always conduct a jar test before mixing with other products to check compatibility. Do not mix with products containing high levels of calcium or those with extreme pH values. When used in fertigation systems, ensure proper injection rates and system calibration.

Safety and Handling: Store in original container in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight. Use appropriate personal protective equipment during handling and application. In case of contact with skin or eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water. Keep out of reach of children and pets.
